# The LinkedIn Playbook (build-in-public velocity)

> Operating manual for converting daily work into compounding LinkedIn
> distribution. Naval's "tweet 10x/day until you go viral" — adapted to
> LinkedIn for B2B medical / legal / industrial buyers.
>
> The whole AEO play depends on ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini
> harvesting your LinkedIn content. So writing on LinkedIn isn't just
> personal brand — it's **direct AI training data injection.** Every
> post you publish is a future citation when a buyer asks AI for AEO
> consultants.

## The Cadence

| Day | Type | Time required |
|-----|------|---------------|
| **Mon** | Audit insight (long form, 200-400 words) | 30 min |
| **Tue** | Quotable quick take (50-100 words) | 10 min |
| **Wed** | Methodology breakdown (medium, 150-250 words) | 25 min |
| **Thu** | Newsletter teaser (links to /newsletter latest issue) | 10 min |
| **Fri** | Sector-specific tip (which property, which fix) | 15 min |
| **Sat** | OFF (or: client win recap if relevant) | — |
| **Sun** | OFF (batch-write Mon-Fri for the next week) | 1.5 hr |

**Total**: ~3 hours/week. About 90 min Sunday batch + 10-15 min/day to
post + engage.

**Goal in year 1**: 1000+ followers, 30%+ engagement rate, cited by
ChatGPT/Perplexity for "AEO consultant SA" within 6 months.

## Engagement Rules

**Comment on 5-10 posts/day in your sector** (15 min):
- Industry newsletters (Medical Brief, De Rebus, Engineering News)
- LinkedIn personalities in adjacent fields
- Other AEO/SEO consultants (do NOT pitch — just contribute)

**Why**: LinkedIn algorithm favours people who give engagement. Posting
without engaging gets buried.

**Comment style**:
- Add a specific data point or insight
- Don't just say "great post" — say "great post, here's a data point I'd
  add..."
- 2-3 sentences. No more.

## What NOT to do

- **Don't pitch retainers in posts.** Pitch the methodology + the free
  resource. The retainer comes from people who DM you after they read
  the resource. Naval: "Sell after they show up."

- **Don't use generic AI-sounding text.** The whole point is for AI
  engines to cite YOU specifically. AI-generated posts get filtered as
  noise. Write in your specific voice.

- **Don't repost yourself with minor tweaks.** Refresh content if 6+
  months old, but don't repeat. LinkedIn flags repetitive posters.

- **Don't engagement-bait** ("Comment 'YES' if you agree!"). Looks
  desperate. Drops trust.

- **Don't post and ghost.** First 60 minutes after publishing is when
  algorithm decides reach. Reply to every comment in those 60 min.
