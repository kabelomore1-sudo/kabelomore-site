## Posting strategy (this is for Kabelo, not for the LinkedIn UI)

**Cadence:** post weekly, minimum. Twice a week if you can sustain it.

**Format mix (each post should be ONE of):**
- A real AI scan screenshot (yours or a client's, anonymised if needed)
- A before/after data point from OMS
- A technical explainer (what schema markup does, why citations matter)
- A client question answered publicly
- A build-in-public update (what shipped on kabelomore.com this week)

**Naval principle:** *document the work, don't perform expertise.* The OMS public case study is the content engine — milk every data point. One Day-1 baseline scan = 4 separate posts (one per AI engine). One Day-30 rescan = 4 more posts. One Day-60 = 4 more. Single client engagement should produce 30+ posts of compounding content.

**Don't post about:**
- Generic "5 AI tips for 2026" listicles (everyone does these)
- Other people's case studies (they don't compound your authority)
- Hot takes on AI ethics (off-message, low conversion)
